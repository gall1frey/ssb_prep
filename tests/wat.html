Rules

  • Grab a paper and a pen and get ready.
  • When you press "GO", you will be shown a blank screen for 5 seconds.
  • Following that, a word will be shown on the screen for 15 seconds
  • You are supposed to write a sentence comprising of that word in the given time.
  • After the time is up, a new word shall be shown.
  • This cycle will continue for 60 words.
  • After the time is up, an alert tone will be played and you will be redirected to a page with all the questions. Save these questions somewhere so you can analyse your answers later.
  • Test once started, cannot be stopped/paused.
  • To ensure the experience is as close to actual SSB as possible, no timers have been put up on the screen. It is advised that you do not use a watch/timer as you attempt this as well, since it won't be allowed in real life.
  • Press F11 to enter fullscreen mode to activate the "GO" button.
